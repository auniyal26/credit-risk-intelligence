# SQL Risk Findings v1

## 1. Contract type
Cash loans show higher default risk (8.35%) than revolving loans (5.48%).

## 2. Income band
Higher income generally corresponds to lower default risk, with the 300k+ group showing the lowest default rate (5.95%).

## 3. Age bucket
Default risk declines with age. Applicants under 25 show the highest default rate (12.31%), while applicants 55+ show the lowest (5.21%).

## 4. Employment length
Shorter employment history is associated with higher risk. Applicants with <1 year or 1–3 years of employment show the highest default rates (~11%), while 10y+ is much lower (5.19%).

## 5. Bureau overdue history
Applicants with bureau overdue history are the highest-risk segment identified so far (16.20%), versus 7.62% for those without bureau overdue history.
